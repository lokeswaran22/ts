# Employee Features Guide

## ‚úÖ What Employees Can Do:

### 1. **Add Activities to Timesheet**
- Click on any **empty time slot** (shows "+")
- Click on any **existing activity** to edit it
- Fill in:
  - Activity Type (Epub, Proof, Calibr, Meeting, Break, Lunch, Other)
  - Description/Notes
  - Page numbers (for Epub, Proof, Calibr)
- Click "Save Activity"

### 2. **Request Leave**
- Click on **your name** in the first column
- Click "Mark Leave" button
- Choose:
  - Full Day Leave (checkbox), OR
  - Specific time slots (From/To dropdowns)
- Click "Submit"

### 3. **Request Permission**
- Click on **your name** in the first column
- Click "Mark Permission" button
- Select time slots (From/To)
- Enter reason
- Click "Submit"

### 4. **View Your Timesheet**
- See only YOUR row
- View your daily totals (Proof, Epub, Calibr pages)
- Navigate dates using ‚Üê Today ‚Üí buttons
